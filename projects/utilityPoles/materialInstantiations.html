What exactly is it that these poles offer? Or what is likely the more pertinent question: what do we take from them? To reduce them solely to objects of study or to 'instantiations' of theoretical power can elide investigation into what is already indicated by their name: utility. These objects were not created to serve as material for academic discourse nor do they seem to be loci of much theoretical concern. However, these structures were created with much thought, planning, and theory, and exist already as crucial to much of our material worlds.
