Utility poles carry power across our freeways and roads, from home to home. As objects of transference and connectivity, these infrastructures offer various sites of study for material instantiations of power. This site will explore those forms of movement and transfer, and the tangle of wires through which these occur.

The title "HIGH VOLTAGE" is pulled from one of many utility pole tags. Wikipedia defines high voltage as "electrical energy at voltages high enough to inflict harm on living organisms".* Electrotechnik defines high voltage as ranging from 35kV to 230 kV.*
